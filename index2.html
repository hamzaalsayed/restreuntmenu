Meal 1

Kabab Halabi

Kabab Halabi features grilled skewers of marinated ground lamb or beef, infused with aromatic spices. Juicy and flavorful, it's served with pita bread, garlic sauce, and a fresh salad, embodying the essence of Middle Eastern cuisine in a deliciously satisfying meal.

150 egp

Meal 2

Kabab El Farah

Kabab El Farah: Tender skewers of marinated lamb or beef, grilled to perfection, accompanied by fragrant rice, grilled vegetables, and a tangy tahini sauce. This celebratory dish bursts with rich flavors, making it perfect for special gatherings

200 egp

Meal 3

Royal Mixed Grill Feast

The "Royal Mixed Grill Feast" is a grand showcase of Middle Eastern flavors, featuring expertly grilled meats. It includes lamb chops, marinated and grilled to perfection with aromatic spices, shish tawook, which are tender chicken pieces with a garlic hint, and kofta kebabs, minced meat skewers mixed with fresh herbs. The platter is completed with beef kebabs, offering a juicy interior with a beautifully charred exterior. This combination of meats delivers a rich, flavorful experience perfect for any grill lover.

450 egp
